Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of agricultural products including fresh fruits, vegetables, organic products, and daily necessities. We also provide mobile application development services for businesses.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we provide international shipping services. Our logistics network covers over 50 countries worldwide. Contact us for specific shipping information to your location.

How can I become a partner?

We welcome partnership inquiries from distributors, suppliers, and businesses in related industries. Please email us at lina@bjbingtang.com with your partnership proposal.

Are your mobile apps available on both platforms?

Yes, our mobile applications are available on both Google Play Store and Apple App Store. You can download them using the links on our homepage.

What is your response time?

We typically respond to inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please indicate the urgency in your message.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, we offer custom agricultural product sourcing and mobile application development solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
